05 · Objections

Every objection has been answered before.

"We already have analytics"
Your dashboard shows campaign ROAS — that's batting average. Forge shows which variable inside the campaign drove that ROAS. That's OBP.
"AI can generate 100 ads in 4 minutes"
Great. Now you have 100 ads and no idea which variable made the winner win. Next month you generate 100 more — still guessing. Forge tells AI what to make next.
"Our agency handles creative"
Agencies optimize campaigns. Forge isolates variables. Different layers. Forge makes the agency more effective.
"Sample size too small?"
Every campaign adds data. By 30, you have benchmarks. Architecture matters more than starting volume.
"Can't we just A/B test?"
A/B tests compare two ads. Forge isolates which variable won — and applies it to every future ad.
